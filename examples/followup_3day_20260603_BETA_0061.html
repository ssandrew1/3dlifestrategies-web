3D Life Strategies: Body-Mind-Spirit · Action Plan

Your Action Items for Transformation

A focused 90-day roadmap to reconnect with your body, reclaim your professional identity, and rebuild your sense of purpose and joy.

⚡ Body — 2/5
🧠 Mind — 2/5
✝ Spirit — 2/5
Priority Actions by Domain
🏃
Body
Physical Foundation
  • Begin a 15–20 minute daily walk, 3–4 times per week during available school hours
  • Move bedtime 30 minutes earlier to target 7+ hours of sleep per night
  • Research and plan one affordable, nutritious weekly meal using beans or eggs as protein
  • Download the Flipp app to compare local grocery prices and plan budget-friendly meals
  • Commit to one simple stretching or bodyweight routine at home, 2 times per week
  • Schedule your annual health check-up and discuss sleep quality with your doctor
  • Replace one sugary beverage per day with water to build a sustainable hydration habit
💡
Mind
Career & Learning
  • Update or create a simple one-page résumé using a free template from Canva.com
  • Listen to one career or job-return podcast episode per week during household chores
  • Identify 3–5 target job types that align with your skills and family schedule needs
  • Research local workforce development centers and free job coaching resources
  • Take one free short course on Google Career Certificates or LinkedIn Learning basics
  • Draft a confident 2–3 sentence explanation of your career gap for interview practice
  • Apply to one well-researched position per week rather than applying broadly
✝
Spirit
Connection & Purpose
  • Schedule a monthly coffee or walk with one neighborhood mom or friend
  • Text or call your sister one genuine, meaningful message each week
  • Create one weekly family ritual—Friday movie night, Sunday pancakes, or family walk
  • Write down or mentally note 2–3 things you are grateful for each morning or evening
  • Reclaim one small piece of your former self—a walk with music you love, 10 minutes to read
  • Identify one person in your life or through media who can serve as a steady mentor
  • Practice noticing moments of ordinary connection with your kids and husband as meaningful
Books to Read
Mind
You Are a Badass
Jen Sincero, 2013
This book directly addresses the belief that life cannot change. Written in a warm, humorous tone, it speaks to anyone who has underestimated their own capacity to create transformation—exactly where you are right now.
Spirit
The Gifts of Imperfection
Brené Brown, 2010
Brown explores how caregivers and mothers suppress their own worth. Her research-based storytelling validates your experience and offers practical tools to reclaim your sense of value and belonging.
Body
The Circadian Code
Dr. Satchin Panda, 2019
This book explains how the timing of eating and sleeping—not just the content—affects energy and mood. It offers cost-free tools that work within your budget constraints.
Body
Spark: The Revolutionary New Science of Exercise and the Brain
John Ratey, 2008
Ratey provides compelling evidence that even modest aerobic activity like brisk walking dramatically improves mood, anxiety, and focus—exactly the benefits you need most right now.
Mind
What Color Is Your Parachute?
Richard N. Bolles, updated annually
The world's most trusted job-search guide includes specific chapters on returning to work after a gap, identifying transferable skills, and finding roles that fit your life. Practical, step-by-step, and affordable.
Mind
Pivot
Jenny Blake, 2016
Blake presents a low-risk method for career transitions by building on existing strengths. Perfect for someone who feels paralyzed by "I don't know where to start" and already has real, marketable skills.
Spirit
The Power of Now
Eckhart Tolle, 1997
Tolle teaches finding peace in the present moment rather than waiting for future circumstances to improve. Learning to find contentment today will reduce daily burden and increase your resilience.
Spirit
Present Over Perfect
Shauna Niequist, 2016
Written for people carrying caregiver burnout, Niequist offers warm, story-driven permission to slow down and reconnect with what matters most—without guilt or pressure.

90-Day Foundation Milestones
🏃
Body
Establish a consistent walking habit (15–20 min, 3+ times per week) and improve sleep by moving bedtime 30 min earlier. Small wins in physical energy compound quickly.
💼
Mind
Complete a polished résumé, research 5–10 target positions, and apply to 3–4 well-fit opportunities. Position yourself actively in the job market with clarity and confidence.
🤝
Connection
Establish one weekly family ritual and one monthly friendship connection. Build the relational anchors that make daily stress bearable and joy accessible.
✨
Purpose
Shift from "no path forward" to "one clear next step." Feel the difference a small daily practice of gratitude and intentional rest makes in your overall sense of possibility.
