The 3D Life Strategies Framework:


The 3D Life Strategies approach is built upon the premise that every individual possesses God-given gifts and talents. The framework operates through a specific process of personal discovery that also nurtures processes for life-long learning and ways to implement utilizing generative ai.


The 3D Process

  • Discovery: Identifying one's personality, character, and KSAs (Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities).
  • Decision: Making the choice to move forward with these identified traits.
  • Development: Ideally working with a coach or mentor to establish SMART Goals that turn latent abilities into active development.

The Steps

  1. Understanding 3D: One of the first steps is to go through a slide show of 3D in more detail so you know how all the steps and exercises fit together.
  2. Passion & Purpose: Use the 3D Life Design & Goal Framework explorer to remind yourself of what makes you smile. Learning, Laughing, Football, ... This is just an AI exploration tool you can spend 5 minutes on or 5 hours. It includes topics: "Adventure, Relationships, Learning, Health & Vitality, Creativity, Service ..."
  3. Personality Matters: So we provide a test that reveals key factors of your personality, character, and KSAs (Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities). Understanding your natural tendencies is the first step in Stewardship. This assessment measures the "Big Five" traits to help us align your KSAs with your God-given purpose.
  4. Survey Questions: The 4th step is where the main 3D survey questions are filled out. This along with your personality assessment is used with AI to create the "Discovery" Report. Once the survey is submitted, it will be emailed to the related party or coach.
    NOTE: Garbage-in → Garbage-out. So, your journey / discovery is only as good or honest as you make it.
  5. Report Review: Ideally, if working with a coach or mentor, you sit with them and have an honest discussion about your findings, goals, and areas to grow. The Report is based solely on your inputs and personality values provided. It will provide summaries, suggested books, and AI prompts to help you "Decide" what to "Develop" next on your 3D journey.
