The Founders

Visionary & Founder

Neal Hayes

He is an ordained chaplain and certified life coach who helps individuals and teams grow with purpose and clarity. With a background in medical sales, family business leadership, and staff development, he brings a practical approach to growth.

As the founder of 3D Life Strategies, Neal created The 3D Playbook—a holistic framework for Body, Mind, and Spirit as the foundation for meaningful life change.

Neal has served as a workplace chaplain and character coach, walking alongside people through pivotal life moments with a deep commitment to faith and family.

Strategic Operations - LinkedIn

Jay Monson

Following a 40-year career in private optometry and multiple business ventures, Jay entered his "second act" with a calling to use his experience and faith to give back.

Jay developed a passion for encouraging professionals to thoughtfully pursue what God may have next for them—especially those with the desire to make a lasting difference.

After seeing Neal present the 3D Playbook, Jay saw its potential for navigating transitions with clarity. He now guides the vision into a sustainable platform that serves people across all stages of life.

Steve Andrew

Steve brings a blend of science and technology to the team. With a background in chemistry and forensics, he spent 30 years as a software architect at Oracle designing complex systems.

Today, Steve focuses on Artificial Intelligence, transforming the original concept into a structured, actionable system: Discover, Decide, Develop.

He designed the platform itself, integrating personality insights into AI-driven reports to deliver personalized guidance and practical prompts for every user.

Our Story

3D Life Strategies began over a decade ago as a teaching framework. It was a vision to help people align their physical, mental, and spiritual health to find their true path.

When Jay encountered this teaching, he recognized its potential for those searching for renewed purpose. Then Steve joined, bringing systems thinking that turned the concept into a digital reality.

Evolution of the Vision

  • 1

    The Process: Clearly defined steps of Discover, Decide, and Develop.

  • 2

    The Assessment: A structured experience to gauge current life alignment.

  • 3

    The Intelligence: AI-generated reports providing personalized insights.

  • 4

    The Result: Practical tools to guide ongoing growth and intentional action.
