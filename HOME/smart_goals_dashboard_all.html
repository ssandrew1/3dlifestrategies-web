📚 Goal Setting Guide

What is SMART?

SMART is a proven framework for setting goals that are clear, achievable, and motivating. Each letter represents a key element:

Specific - Clearly define what you want to achieve
Measurable - Establish concrete criteria for measuring progress
Achievable - Set goals that are realistic and attainable
Relevant - Ensure the goal matters to you and aligns with your values
Time-bound - Set a deadline or timeframe for completion

What is SMET?

SMET is an enhanced version of SMART that emphasizes the "why" behind your goals. It includes all elements of SMART plus:

Empowering - Your goal should energize and inspire you. It's about recognizing why achieving this goal matters and how it will improve your life.

When you understand not just what you want to achieve, but why it empowers you, you're more likely to stay committed and overcome obstacles.

Why Goal Setting Matters

Direction: Clear goals give you a roadmap for your efforts, helping you focus on what truly matters rather than drifting aimlessly.

Motivation: When you see progress toward a meaningful goal, you feel more energized and committed. Small wins build momentum and confidence.

Accountability: Written goals create a contract with yourself. They help you track progress and celebrate milestones, reinforcing positive habits.

Growth: Pursuing well-defined goals stretches you beyond your comfort zone, building resilience, skills, and self-belief. Over time, you become someone who achieves what they set out to do.

Integration: The 3D approach—Body, Mind, Spirit—ensures holistic growth. You're not just achieving tasks; you're building a whole, balanced life.

Generate a habit-tracking calendar I can print out and mark with checkmarks or stickers.

Make a motivational checklist with mini-goals and rewards for completing this SMART goal.
Design a reflection worksheet to capture what I learned and how to improve next week.

Create a printable dashboard that shows my progress, streaks, and overall completion rate.

How to Use This Dashboard

4. Track Progress: Use the generated documents to monitor your daily progress and stay accountable.
